What's inside

Features

Every system built around the fight. Roll your fate, master your species, dominate the leaderboards.

🎲
Species Rolling

Roll for one of 20+ species from common Half-Blood (26%) to legendary Demi God (0.5%). Each roll is permanent until you switch.

⚔️
Turn-Based Combat

Full RPG fights with attack, heal, and ULT actions. Every species has a unique passive and ultimate that completely changes your playstyle.

🤖
Bot Fights

Challenge AI opponents across 4 difficulties: Baby Bot, Warrior Bot, Demon Bot, and the terrifying God Bot. Earn leaderboard points for winning.

🏆
Leaderboards

Persistent server rankings for fight wins and bomb game victories. Compete for the top spot and prove your dominance.

🌑
Quest System

Complete the Reaper Quest to unlock the legendary Reaper species. Progress is saved and carries across sessions.

⚡
Awakenings

Push your Cyborg to its limits — 25 wins, 500 damage, 15 ULT uses — and awaken into the transcendent Mechangel.

🔄
Class Switching

Switch species every 3 hours. Some rolls are worth rerolling for — balance strategy with luck.

💾
Persistent Channels

Set a dedicated duel channel for your server. All fight requests route there automatically.

About the Bot

Legends of the Rift is a Discord RPG bot built entirely from scratch — no templates, no frameworks, just a vision and the desire to build something worth playing. It features a full combat engine, 20+ unique species with hand-crafted abilities, a progression system with quests and awakenings, and multiple game modes. Every line of code was written by Teal.
